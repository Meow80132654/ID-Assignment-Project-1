About us

How this started

During the 2019 covid 19 pandemic, everyone could not go about as often anymore. As such my mother decided to pick up a few hobbies. With time on her hand, one of these hobbies happened to be jewelry making which she realised she had a talent for. Along with the positive feedback shes has received from friends who appreciate her craftsmanship, we're attempting to make this a small side venture in order to monetise her hobby and make a few quick bucks.

What's for sale?

Necklace

Necklaces are the most in stock product and our main specialisation. With over 10 different necklaces to choose from. Custom orders are also possible.

Bracelet

There are many types of bracelets in the world, the bracelets we usually make the most are Tennis bracelets and pearl bracelets. Sorry to disappoint you if you were looking for something else but you can try ordering a custom bracelet.

Custom

We take custom orders, just fill in your contact details and we can start chatting to understand what you want to commission. Please note that we have the right to choose what commissions to take.
